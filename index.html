About

I have more than 15 years of teaching and research experience. I have guided more than 50 M. Tech. and B.Tech. dissertations and presently guiding 02 Ph. D. students. I have published more than 30 journal and conference papers in the area of image processing, pattern recognition, data mining, and soft computing. I was the part of successfully completed DRDE funded project on image analysis and currently running a project funded by SERB-DST (New Delhi) on Histopathological Image Analysis. I have been an active member of many organizing committees of various conferences and workshops. I was also a guest editor of International journal of Swarm Intelligence. My research areas include Image Processing, Pattern Recognition, Data Mining, and Soft Computing.
